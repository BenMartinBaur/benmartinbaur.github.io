## Core Promise
Readers will know how to detect, frame, and recover from failure without damaging trust.

## Key Points To Cover
- Failure signals appear earlier than most teams admit.
- Recovery quality is often more career-defining than initial failure.
- How transparency affects trust in technical leadership.
- Why postmortems should improve systems, not punish people.

## Story Anchor Prompts
- A high-pressure failure and the first corrective step that changed trajectory.
- A recovery story where accountability improved reputation.

## Anti-Pattern Section
"Blame choreography": protecting image instead of fixing systems.

## Framework Block
Recovery Loop:
1. Signal detection
2. Root-cause framing
3. Ownership statement
4. System-level corrective action
5. Learning transfer

## 15-Minute Reader Exercise
Have readers draft a one-page recovery memo for a recent setback using the Recovery Loop.

## CTA
"What is one lesson from a recent miss that your team should reuse?"

## Draft Checklist
- Include one vulnerable story
- Keep accountability language balanced and factual
- Avoid perfection narratives
- End with a reusable template
