## Core Promise
Readers will leave with a decision model they can use immediately for role and skill planning.

## Key Points To Cover
- Why both depth and breadth matter, but not at the same ratio at all times.
- Signals that indicate "go deeper now".
- Signals that indicate "expand scope now".
- Trade-offs and hidden costs of each choice.

## Story Anchor Prompts
- A moment where deep specialization paid off.
- A moment where broad context unlocked influence.

## Anti-Pattern Section
"Trend hopping": switching domains too fast and compounding nothing.

## Framework Block
Decision Grid:
- Market relevance
- Unique leverage
- Time-to-competence
- Energy fit
- Compounding potential

## 15-Minute Reader Exercise
Ask readers to evaluate one current opportunity with the Decision Grid and choose a 6-month bet.

## CTA
"Are you in a depth season or a breadth season right now, and why?"
