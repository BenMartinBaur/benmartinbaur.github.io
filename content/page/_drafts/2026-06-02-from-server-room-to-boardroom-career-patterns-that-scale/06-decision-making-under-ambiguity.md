## Core Promise
Readers get a repeatable way to make and communicate quality decisions under uncertainty.

## Key Points To Cover
- Why ambiguity is a normal operating condition in modern tech.
- Distinguish reversible vs irreversible decisions.
- How to define decision quality separately from outcome luck.
- How to communicate assumptions and risk transparently.

## Story Anchor Prompts
- A difficult call made with partial data and what was learned later.
- A delayed decision that cost more than a fast imperfect one.

## Anti-Pattern Section
"Analysis paralysis": waiting for certainty while opportunity decays.

## Framework Block
A.R.C. Decision Model:
- Assumptions
- Reversibility
- Consequence window

## 15-Minute Reader Exercise
Have readers apply A.R.C. to one pending decision and commit to a date-bound next step.

## CTA
"Which decision are you over-delaying right now because you want certainty?"
