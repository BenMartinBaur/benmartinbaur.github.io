# From Server Room to Boardroom: Career Patterns That Scale

Subline: Learnings from Pinnical Award Winner - Ben Baur.

## Series Objective
Build a 10-post career series that translates repeatable high-performance patterns into practical, inclusive actions for enterprise architects, senior ICs, and aspiring leaders.

## Brand Alignment Rules
- Primary pillar: real
- Secondary support: strategy
- Voice: first-person, practical, non-preachy
- Framing: "Here is what I learned" not "Here is what you should do"
- Include one practical framework or exercise in every post
- Use only approved tags

## Audience Segments
- Aspiring tech leaders (IC to influence)
- Enterprise architects and principal-track ICs
- Senior leaders building high-performance teams

## Publishing Cadence
- Rhythm: 1 post every 2 weeks
- Window: June to October 2026
- Total: 10 posts
- Blog cadence fit: This series can run as one of two monthly posts while preserving room for Build pillar posts.

## Editorial Structure For Every Post
1. Story hook from lived experience
2. Pattern decoded
3. Anti-pattern to avoid
4. Framework or checklist
5. 15-minute reader exercise
6. CTA question for comments and LinkedIn
